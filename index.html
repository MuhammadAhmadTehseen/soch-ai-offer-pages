Common questions

Everything you're wondering about.

Is this a course, or do you actually set things up?+
We set things up. Claude is installed on every device and connected to your Google Workspace during the foundation day — the teaching happens on your live setup, not slides. You end day one with working tools, not homework.
How big does our firm need to be?+
The Setup Day fits firms of 1–50 people; the Capability Sprint fits firms of up to 100 with two or more distinct functions. On-site sessions hold up to 30 people — larger firms split into two sessions or route extra departments through the remote labs.
What do we need before you arrive?+
Google Workspace (Drive, Sheets, Gmail, Calendar) and a decision-maker in the room. That's it — we handle installation, configuration and accounts on the day.
Is it fully on-site?+
The foundation day is on-site in Tallinn, led by Rizwan. In the Sprint, weeks 2–4 run remotely — live build labs, an async brief, and a demo day — with Soch's technical team delivering throughout.
What happens if someone misses a session?+
Sprint sessions are structured so departments work in parallel — a missed person catches up inside their department's track, and the week-3 brief is async by design. For the on-site day we agree the attendee list in advance so the right people are in the room.
What about our client data?+
Everything runs inside your own Google Workspace and your own Claude accounts — Soch doesn't take copies of your files. We'll walk your decision-maker through the data-handling setup before anything is connected.
Can you help with the grant paperwork?+
We'll point you to the right measure (roadmap grant, development-activities grant, or the coming AI voucher) and describe the program in the format applications expect. Eligibility and the application itself stay with your firm.
What happens after the program ends?+
Every automation has a named internal owner and a metric, and your team keeps the full handover pack. Firms that want to go further — deeper automations, agents running autonomously — scope that as a follow-on engagement.
